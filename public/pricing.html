FAQ

Common questions

What counts as an "AI run"?

Each AI run is one significant action — drafting a memo, adapting a template, generating a petition section, or summarizing a meeting. Searching, reading, and editing don't count.

Can I switch plans?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. The change takes effect at the next billing cycle, and unused AI runs don't carry over.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — every plan starts with a 14-day trial. No credit card required. Get in touch and we'll set you up.

Is my client data secure?

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never use client documents to train models. Enterprise plans include on-premise deployment for firms that need it.

Tell us about your practice — we'll recommend a plan that makes sense.
